Privacy Policy for Neuralhub

We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy policy.

Information We Collect

2.1 Data Provided by Users: Users provide datasets through CSV files or other formats. This data is solely used for the purpose of designing, training, and simulating AI networks and is not shared with any third parties.

2.2 Authentication Data: We utilize Google Auth for sign-in purposes. We only fetch and store the basic profile information required for authentication.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Neuralhub.
  • To train and evaluate neural networks based on data provided by the user.
  • To provide real-time feedback and updates during the model training process.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to ensure the protection of user data. The background service for AI processing terminates within an hour if there's no activity to ensure minimal data retention.

Data Retention

We store the user datasets only as long as necessary for the processing and training of neural networks. Post processing, datasets are purged from our servers to ensure user data privacy.

Third-Party Services

Our platform uses Google Auth for authentication purposes. We recommend users review Google's Privacy Policy for clarity on how they manage user data.

User Rights

Users can request to view, modify, or delete their data stored on Neuralhub at any time. Users can also request a halt in data processing or revoke their consent for data processing.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes made will be communicated to our users in due course.

Contact Us

For any queries regarding this Privacy Policy, users can contact us at sam@neuralhub.ai.
